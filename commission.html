TERMS OF SERVICE

If you want to buy a commission from me personally, there are a few rules you're going to need to know to remain on my good side! These rules are as follows:
  1. Do not suggest an alternate method of payment--I will only accept payments through PayPal or Google Pay!
  2. Do not negotiate
  3. Do not be disrespectful
  4. Do not write an essay on your commission--keep it brief!
  5. Do not skimp on references--provide every angle of a character possible
  6. Do not commission my character from another artist without permission

Upon breaking any of these rules, you will be warned the first handful of times. If you continue to do so after numerous warnings, I can--and will--refuse/decline service to you! You will be issued a refund and recieve a temporary ban until the next batch. I reserve my rights to do this.

In short; respect my time and I will respect yours. Otherwise, we will have a problem.
